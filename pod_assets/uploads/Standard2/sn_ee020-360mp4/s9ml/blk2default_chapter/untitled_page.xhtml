Learning Outcomes

After studying this chapter and completing the assessment, you will be able to

  1. 2.1
    Cite some examples of how unethical behavior in our world has negatively affected business practices.
  2. 2.2
    Distinguish among ethics, morals, and values. Cite influences on group and individual values.
  3. 2.3
    Discuss the relationship between law and ethics.
  4. 2.4
    Discuss the different types of ethical reasoning and provide examples of responses to ethical issues by business firms, educational institutions, governments, and trade and professional associations.
  5. 2.5
    Discuss some ways businesses can ensure ethical practices.
